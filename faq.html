Frequently Asked Questions

What is your business model?

We partner with apartment owners to co‑invest in renovations and manage the sale through our brokerage. Profits are shared after repayment of renovation costs.

Do I need to pay anything upfront?

No. We fund the renovation. Our investment is recouped when the apartment is sold, and we split the remaining profit.

How long does the process take?

A typical renovation and sale cycle takes three to six months, depending on the scope of work and market conditions.

Who handles permits and design?

Our in‑house team secures permits, plans the design and manages construction. You can be as involved as you wish without any administrative burden.

Do I have to use your brokerage to sell?

Yes. Our full‑service model includes listing the renovated apartment through our licensed brokerage to ensure a smooth sale and maximize returns.
